Area

Australia is a country in Oceania bordering the Indian Ocean and the Southern Pacific Ocean.

The terrain is mostly low plateau with deserts.

Australian values based on freedom, respect, fairness and equality of opportunity are central to our community remaining a secure, prosperous and peaceful place to live.

A “no worries” attitude. There's something Aussies are known the world over for, and that's their easy-going, friendly attitude. Of course food, events, art and history are all vital parts of the Australian culture, but what really distinguishes an Aussie is his or her laid-back outlook on life.
