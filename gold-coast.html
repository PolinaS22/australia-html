Gold Coast

About

The Gold Coast is one of Australia's premier holiday destinations. Famous for its surf breaks, stunning beaches, theme parks, shopping, nightlife and hinterland, it has been attracting Australian families for many decades.

Surfing is everywhere on the Gold Coast. You can see it in surf-themed cafe menus, surfboard-shaped park benches and surf reports on the nightly news.

Whether you’re an adrenaline junkie, a film buff, a nature-lover or a water baby, the Gold Coast’s famous theme parks have something for you. Dreamworld, Wet’n’Wild, Movie World and Paradise Country are all just a short drive from the Gold Coast.

Best Gold Coast's beaches

Surfers Paradise Beach

Image the subtle waves kissing the white sands and sea as far as the horizon. The iconic skyline and golden beaches of Surfers Paradise is the Gold Coast’s postcard image to Australia and the world. Visitors can also enjoy a wide variety of activities such as beach volleyball, surfing, and parasailing. Surfers Paradise Beach puts up several events all year round, such as the Australian Beach Games and the Sand Safari Arts Festival, making it a year-round destination for tourists.

Burleigh Heads Beach

Golden sands, good surf near the rocks for serious surfers, great small waves for body boarding and life guards to keep everyone safe. If you take the path through the headland to Talebudgeera Creek you will be treated to the best creek for swimming and snorkeling. Burleigh Head National Park is adjacent to the beach, offering visitors the opportunity to take a relaxing walk along the several walking trails.

Coolangatta Beach

It is famous for its clear blue waters, soft white sands, and scenic views. The beach is clean, safe, and patrolled by lifeguards. The beach is also connected to nearby beaches such as Kirra Beach, Greenmount Beach, and Rainbow Bay, creating a long stretch of almost continuous sandy coast that visitors can enjoy.

Kirra Beach

This beach is a favorite destination for surfers due to its consistent sets of impressive waves and clear waters. Kirra Beach is famous for hosting various surfing events throughout the year. Kirra Point which is situated at the northern end of Kirra Beach is a famous spot for surfers.

Greenmount Beach

This family-friendly beach has calm and sheltered waters, making it a great spot for swimming, paddle-boarding, and kayaking. The beach is surrounded by grassy areas and parks that offer perfect picnic spots with barbeque facilities. The nearby walking and cycling trails provide excellent opportunities to explore the beauty of the Gold Coast. It is a perfect beach for visitors looking for a relaxing day out.
