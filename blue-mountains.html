The Blue Mountains are justifiably world famous. It has it's own charmâ€¦ there are few calming places to disconnect from the world and reconnect with yourself.

There are over 900 canyons but only few can be explored. Most of the canyons are deep and narrow.

You may ask: why are the Blue Mountains blue? The reason is simple: eucalyptus oil droplets emitted from the forests combine with dust particles and water vapour, scattering short wavelength rays of light which are predominantly blue in colour.
